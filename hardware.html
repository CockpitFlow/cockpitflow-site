SUPPORTED BOARDS

From Arduino Nano to ESP32

⚡

Arduino Mega 2560

54 digital I/O + 16 analog. Perfect for full cockpit panels. Serial USB at 115200 baud.

📡

Arduino Nano

Compact — 14 digital + 8 analog. Great for small panels (radio stack, switch panel).

📶

ESP32 (WiFi)

Wireless via UDP. No USB cable needed. ESP32, ESP32-S3, ESP32-C6 supported. Flash from the app.

WORKFLOW

From board to cockpit in minutes

1️⃣

Flash

Flash CockpitFlow firmware from the app. Auto-detect board type.

2️⃣

Map

Drag & drop pin mapping. Assign buttons, encoders, pots to sim functions.

3️⃣

Calibrate

Set deadzone, curve (linear/log/exp), min/max for analog inputs.

4️⃣

Fly

Everything works. Live monitor shows pin events in real-time.

PIN TYPES

Every input and output

Buttons & Toggles

Momentary push buttons and on/off toggle switches. Debounced in firmware.

Encoders

Rotary encoders with CW/CCW events. Configurable steps per detent. Perfect for heading, altitude, radio tuning.

Potentiometers

Analog sliders and knobs. 10-bit resolution. Deadzone and curve calibration built-in.

LEDs

Output pins driven by sim state. Gear lights, AP indicators, warning lamps.

Displays

7-segment (TM1637) and OLED displays. Show frequencies, altitude, heading.

Servos & Steppers

Drive physical gauge needles. Map sim values to servo positions.
