FEATURES

Everything you need in the cockpit

CockpitFlow connects your phone, tablet, or custom hardware panel to your flight simulator.

✈

Cockpit Controls

Throttle, mixture, flaps, trim, yoke (touch + gyro), switches, magneto. All on your phone.

☑

Smart Checklists

Pre-flight to shutdown. Strict or smart mode. Auto-detect items from sim data. Community presets for any aircraft.

📈

Live Gauges

IAS, altitude, VS, heading, RPM — live from the sim. Color-coded warnings.

🔌

Hardware Integration

Connect Arduino or ESP32. Map buttons, encoders, potentiometers. Flash firmware from the app.

📄

JSON Presets

Everything is a JSON file. Cockpit layouts, checklists, profiles. Create, share, import.

📱

Mobile Companion

Android app with auto-discovery. Desktop controls what mobile shows. No HTTPS hassle.

🎓

Failure Scenarios

Train for emergencies. Arm engine, electrical, instrument failures with customizable triggers.

📊

Flight Debrief

Landing rate, G-forces, approach quality. Track your progress over time.

🌐

Multi-language

13 languages. English, Romanian, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, and more.

HOW IT WORKS

Up and running in 2 minutes

01

Install CockpitFlow

Download and run the desktop app. It connects to X-Plane or MSFS automatically.

02

Install Mobile App

Install the Android companion. It auto-discovers CockpitFlow on your network.

03

Fly

Cockpit controls and checklists on your phone. Everything synced live.
